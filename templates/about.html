In Rwanda, Cardiovascular diseases (CVDs) are the third leading cause of death(Including Heart Diseases),and are the number 1 cause of death globally, taking an estimated 17.9 million lives each year, which accounts for 31% of all deaths worldwide. Four out of 5CVD deaths are due to heart attacks and strokes, and one-third of these deaths occur prematurely in people under 70 years of age. Heart failure is a common event caused by CVDs.

We beleive that the Early detection with this system can potentially save lives and improve overall the better health within the Rwandan population.
